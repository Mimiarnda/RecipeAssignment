Classic Banana Bread

This quick & easy banana bread recipe tastes DELICIOUS and is just like my mom’s old-fashioned classic banana bread. Perfect for breakfast, dessert, or a snack!

About Banana Bread

Banana bread is proof that magic happens when life gives you overripe bananas. Born in the 1930s during the Great Depression, this cozy classic started as a thrifty way to avoid food waste and quickly rose to fame thanks to its moist texture, sweet flavor, and irresistible aroma.

Over the years, banana bread has taken on endless twists: studded with nuts, swirled with chocolate, or made healthy-ish with oats and honey. But at its core, it’s still that simple, satisfying loaf we turn to when we want something warm, nostalgic, and just a little bit sweet.

Today, banana bread is baked for all kinds of reasons: as a cozy weekend project, a thoughtful homemade gift, or a nostalgic bite of childhood. Whether dressed up with decadent add-ins or enjoyed in its purest form, banana bread remains a symbol of comfort, creativity, and the joy of turning something humble into something truly delicious.

Easy to bake and even easier to love, banana bread isn’t just a treat it’s a mood.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 10–15 minutes

Cooking time: 65–70 minutes

Number of Servings: 12 slices

Difficulty level: Beginner

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. Mash bananas with a fork until smooth.
  3. Slice and soften the butter, then mix with bananas, eggs, and sugar in a large bowl until blended.
  4. Add remaining ingredients and stir until fully mixed.
  5. Optional: Fold in 1 cup of your favorite add-in (e.g., chocolate chips).
  6. Pour batter into a nonstick bread pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 65–70 minutes.
  8. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
  9. Allow to cool completely before slicing and serving.

Helpful Tips

Use ripe bananas with plenty of brown spots for the best flavor. Unripe bananas will make your bread taste bland and less sweet.

Serving Suggestions

This Banana Bread is delicious with:

  • Fresh berries and whipped cream
  • A drizzle of caramel or chocolate sauce
  • A light dusting of powdered sugar

Storage

Store the cake in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, refrigerate for up to 5 days or freeze unfrosted layers for up to 2 months. Thaw at room temperature before serving.
